How does our amazon service work?

Saturn Service's amazon reseller team is a job where you will be given a method to make purchases on amazon without spending any money. Using this process you'll purchase items for us and ship them to our adress which we will resell and give you a cut of the profit, all at no cost for you!

Amazon FAQ

  • How exactly does it work?
  • You will be given a card to buy amazon items with which we will also track for security reasons, and then you ship your items out to us, once we've sold it we pay you a cut of the profit. It doesnt cost you anything to buy the items.
  • If it doesnt cost anything to do why do you need us?
  • As part of the method you can only purchase one item at a time, which means the more people we have buying items simultaneously the more money we can make at any given time.
